## üìÖ Reserveringen

### Booking System

**Via Google Chat:**

- Patriek op de Chat (voor beide locaties)

**Reservering Format:**

```
Room: [Room Name]
Date: [DD-MM-YYYY]
Time: [HH:MM - HH:MM]
Attendees: [Number + internal/external]
Catering: [Yes/No + details]
Setup: [Special requirements]
```

### Reservering Guidelines

**Advance Booking:**

- **Same day**: Tot 11:00 voor middag slots
- **Next day**: Tot 17:00 vorige dag
- **Week ahead**: Altijd mogelijk
- **Recurring**: Max 4 weken vooruit

**Cancellation:**

- **2+ hours notice**: Geen issues
- **< 2 hours**: Notify Patriek op de Chat
- **No-show**: Auto-release na 15 min

## üìã Meeting Etiquette

### Before Your Meeting

- **Test tech** 15 minutes early
- **Prepare materials** - cables, presentations ready
- **Check catering** - confirm special requests
- **Inform reception** - for external guests

### During Your Meeting

- **Start/end on time** - respect next booking
- **Keep noise levels** appropriate for space
- **Use provided tech** - don't bring conflicting equipment
- **Take notes** on whiteboards/flip charts

### After Your Meeting

- **Clean up** - clear table, dispose of materials
- **Tech cleanup** - disconnect devices, reset to default
- **Whiteboard cleanup** - erase unless specifically saved
- **Catering cleanup** - clear dishes and waste
