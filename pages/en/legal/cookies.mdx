# Cookie Policy

**Last updated:** October 18, 2025

This page explains which cookies Kroescontrol uses on docs.kroescontrol.nl and how you can manage them.

## What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files that websites place on your device. They help websites remember who you are and your preferences.

## Which cookies do we use?

### 1. HubSpot Analytics Cookies

We use **HubSpot** to analyze visitor behavior. HubSpot places the following cookies:

| Cookie Name | Purpose | Retention Period |
|-------------|---------|------------------|
| `__hstc` | Main tracking cookie | 13 months |
| `hubspotutk` | Unique visitor ID | 13 months |
| `__hssc` | Session tracking | 30 minutes |
| `__hssrc` | Session detection | Session |
| `messagesUtk` | Chat widget (if active) | 13 months |

**What we track:**
- Which pages you visit
- How long you stay on the site
- Which content is popular
- General navigation patterns

**What we DON'T track:**
- Personal information (name, email)
- Individual employees
- Sensitive information

### 2. Functional Cookies

| Cookie Name | Purpose | Retention Period |
|-------------|---------|------------------|
| `theme` | Dark/light mode preference | 1 year |
| `analytics-opt-out` | Opt-out status (localStorage) | Permanent |
